What Is Cuisine?

Cuisine refers to a specific, characteristic style of cooking, food preparation, and culinary traditions associated with a particular culture, region, or country. It encompasses unique ingredients, techniques, and flavors that reflect local heritage. Common synonyms include cooking, culinary art, and gastronomy.
