Indian Cuisine

Indian cuisine is a diverse, aromatic, and flavorful tapestry of culinary traditions spanning from the Himalayas to the tropical south, known for its extensive use of spices, herbs, and locally sourced ingredients.

Japanese Cuisine

Japanese cuisine (washoku) is a tradition-based culinary art emphasizing seasonal ingredients, refined flavors, and beautiful presentation.

Korean Cuisine

Bold flavors, comforting stews, and signature ingredients like kimchi—pure Korean culinary magic.

Arabic Cuisine

Arabic cuisine is a blend of Mediterranean, Middle Eastern, and North African traditions, known for rice, lamb, chicken, yogurt, olive oil, and fresh herbs like mint and parsley.

Swiss Cuisine

Swiss cuisine is a hearty mix of German, French, and Italian influences—famous for cheese, potatoes, bread, and dairy.
